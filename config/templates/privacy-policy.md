This privacy policy describes how %{domain} ("Nutshell", "we", "us") collects,
protects and uses the personally identifiable information you may provide
through the %{domain} website, its API, or connected services including
BlackEnvelope. The policy also describes the choices available to you regarding
our use of your personal information and how you can access and update this
information.

# What information do we collect?

- **Basic account information**: When you register on Nutshell, you provide a
  username, an email address, and a password. You may also enter additional
  profile information such as a display name and biography, and upload a profile
  picture and header image. The username, display name, biography, profile
  picture, and header image are visible to other Nutshell members.
- **Membership and payment information**: Nutshell is a paid membership
  platform. Payments are processed by Square. We do not store your credit card
  number or payment method details on our servers. We receive confirmation of
  payment status, your customer email address, and subscription identifiers from
  Square to manage your membership. Your membership status (active, canceled,
  past due) is stored on our servers.
- **Posts, following, and community information**: The list of people you follow
  is visible to other members, the same is true for your followers. When you
  submit a post, the date and time is stored as well as the application you
  submitted the post from. Posts may contain media attachments such as pictures
  and videos. Public posts are visible to all Nutshell members. You control the
  visibility of each post you create.
- **End-to-end encrypted messages (BlackEnvelope)**: Nutshell provides access to
  BlackEnvelope for private messaging and group conversations. Messages sent
  through BlackEnvelope are end-to-end encrypted (E2EE). This means that only
  the participants in a conversation can read the message content. Server
  administrators cannot access the content of E2EE messages. We do store
  metadata necessary for message delivery, such as sender and recipient
  identifiers and timestamps.
- **Direct messages on the public feed**: Direct messages sent through the
  Nutshell public feed (not through BlackEnvelope) are stored on the server and
  are accessible to server administrators. For private conversations, we
  recommend using BlackEnvelope.
- **IPs and other metadata**: When you log in, we record the IP address you log
  in from, as well as the name of your browser application. All logged-in
  sessions are available for your review and revocation in the settings. The
  latest IP address used is stored for up to 12 months. We also retain server
  logs which include the IP address of every request to our server.

# What do we use your information for?

Any of the information we collect from you may be used in the following ways:

- To provide the core functionality of Nutshell. You can only interact with
  other people's content and post your own content when you are logged in. For
  example, you may follow other people to view their combined posts in your own
  personalized home timeline.
- To manage your membership status and ensure that only active members have
  access to the platform.
- To aid moderation of the community, for example comparing your IP address with
  other known ones to determine ban evasion or other violations.
- The email address you provide may be used to send you information,
  notifications about other people interacting with your content or sending you
  messages, and to respond to inquiries and other requests or questions.

# How do we protect your information?

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your
personal information when you enter, submit, or access your personal
information. Among other things, your browser session, as well as the traffic
between your applications and the API, are secured with TLS encryption, and your
password is hashed using a strong one-way algorithm. You may enable two-factor
authentication to further secure access to your account.

Private messaging through BlackEnvelope uses end-to-end encryption, meaning
message content is encrypted on your device before being sent and can only be
decrypted by the intended recipients. The server never has access to the
plaintext content of E2EE messages.

# What is our data retention policy?

We will make a good faith effort to:

- Retain server logs containing the IP address of all requests to this server,
  in so far as such logs are kept, no more than 90 days.
- Retain the IP addresses associated with registered users no more than 12
  months.

You can request and download an archive of your content, including your posts,
media attachments, profile picture, and header image.

You may irreversibly delete your account at any time. Note that end-to-end
encrypted messages in BlackEnvelope that have already been delivered to other
participants' devices cannot be recalled by deleting your account.

# Do we use cookies?

Yes. Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to
your computer's hard drive through your web browser (if you allow). These
cookies enable the site to recognize your browser and, if you have a registered
account, associate it with your registered account.

We use cookies to understand and save your preferences for future visits and to
maintain your authenticated session.

# Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally
identifiable information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist
us in operating our platform, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long
as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. Specifically:

- **Square** processes membership payments on our behalf and receives the
  information necessary to complete transactions.
- **DigitalOcean** provides the server infrastructure where Nutshell is hosted.
- **Cloudflare** provides DNS and network security services.

We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to
comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others'
rights, property, or safety.

Nutshell does not federate with other servers. Your content is not distributed
to external servers. Your posts and profile information remain on the Nutshell
platform.

When you authorize an application to use your account, depending on the scope of
permissions you approve, it may access your public profile information, your
following list, your followers, your lists, all your posts, and your favourites.
Applications can never access your email address or password.

# Third-party services

- **Square**: Payment processing. Subject to Square's privacy policy.
- **BlackEnvelope**: End-to-end encrypted messaging, provided as an integrated
  service. E2EE message content is not accessible to Nutshell administrators.

# Site usage by children

Our site, products, and services are directed to people who are at least 18
years old. If you are under the age of 18, do not use this site.
